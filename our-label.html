The Logo

What the label looks like—and what it means

The ClaimCheck certification logo is awarded exclusively to products whose health claims have received a Green rating—meaning the peer-reviewed evidence strongly and consistently supports the claim at the stated dose and mechanism.

No Orange. No exceptions. Only health claims that clear our full two-axis review—regulatory compliance under DSHEA and scientific validity in the literature—qualify to carry the logo.

The logo comes with a usage license, placement guidelines, and a live public database entry that consumers can look up by scanning or searching. Every logo links back to the full evidence report.

Green-rated health claims only

The logo is never issued for Orange or Red-rated health claims. Its value depends entirely on that standard being maintained.

On-Pack Usage

Where and how you can use it

Once certified, you receive a full usage license with placement guidelines. Here is where the logo can appear.

Product packaging

Display the logo on the front or back panel of your packaging, adjacent to the certified health claim. Clear-space and minimum size guidelines are included in the license kit.

Website & e-commerce

Use the logo on your product pages, brand website, and retailer listings. A digital badge kit with multiple formats (SVG, PNG, dark/light variants) is provided on certification.

Marketing & advertising

The logo may appear in print ads, social media creative, email campaigns, and trade show materials. Usage must reference the specific certified health claim it corresponds to.

Usage boundaries. The logo may only be used in direct association with the specific certified health claim. It cannot be used as a general brand quality mark, applied to uncertified health claims on the same product, or displayed in a way that implies certification of the entire product rather than the specific evaluated claim.

Health Influencer Network

We don’t just certify you.
We amplify you.

ClaimCheck maintains an active network of health and wellness content creators, registered dietitians, and science communicators who specifically promote products with independently verified health claims.

When your product earns a Green certification, it is automatically shared with our influencer network as a brand that has done the scientific work. This gives your certified product organic reach among an audience that values evidence-based health—exactly the consumers who read labels carefully and respond to verified claims.

Unlike paid partnerships, our network recommendations are driven entirely by certification status. Influencers in our network do not promote uncertified products through our platform, which means a recommendation carries genuine credibility signal.

Database feature

Your certified product gets a dedicated entry in our public database, searchable by consumers and referenced by health professionals. The full evidence report is publicly accessible and linked to your product page.

Network announcement

New Green certifications are announced across our influencer and professional network. Creators who cover your product category are specifically notified and given access to the full evaluation report to reference in their content.

Ongoing visibility

Certified products remain featured in our network communications as long as the certification is active. Annual re-evaluations ensure the science stays current—and your network visibility continues.
