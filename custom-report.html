How it works

What happens after you submit

1

We receive your submission

Your product details are logged and assigned to a reviewer within one business day.

2

We review the scientific literature

Our team searches peer-reviewed databases for clinical trials, meta-analyses, and systematic reviews relevant to each claim and ingredient at the stated dose.

3

We score and grade the product

Each claim receives an individual evidence score. The product receives an overall label grade (A to C) reflecting the strength and consistency of the evidence.

4

We publish the report and email you

The full evaluation is added to our public database. You receive an email with a direct link to the report, including all cited studies and our justification for the score.

Reports are typically completed within 5–10 business days. All evaluations are published publicly, regardless of the outcome. ClaimCheck does not accept payment for evaluations and has no financial relationship with manufacturers.
